Common concerns

Questions brands usually ask before working with us

You may already be thinking through some of these. We will cover the ones most relevant to your brand during the call.

Creator Fit

Will creators actually work for our category?

We look at whether the product can be demonstrated clearly, whether creators have a natural angle, and whether commission plus sample economics are strong enough to produce useful content.

Budget

What should we expect to invest?

The real budget is not just ad spend. It includes samples, commissions, promotions, listing work, and management. We will pressure-test whether the numbers support the goal.

Proof

How do we know TikTok Shop can work for us?

Proof has to match your situation. Amazon-native, Shopify-native, niche, seasonal, and early-stage brands each need a different path to signal.

Channel Fit

Why is TikTok Shop different from TikTok ads to Shopify?

TikTok Shop keeps discovery, creator proof, offer, and checkout inside the same platform. That reduces friction, but the listing and creator system still have to be right.

Execution

What does Avanta actually handle?

Creator recruitment, affiliate workflow, listing optimization, content direction, Spark Ads, reporting, and the weekly operating decisions that keep the channel moving.

Readiness

What if our listing, offer, or inventory is not ready?

Then we fix that first or recommend waiting. Weak images, confusing variants, poor bundles, low inventory, or bad margins can make even strong creator attention underperform.
