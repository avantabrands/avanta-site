What we will talk through on the call.

These are the main questions worth thinking about before we speak.

Is TikTok Shop actually a fit for your product?

We will look at your category, price point, margin, inventory, and whether creators can explain the product clearly in short-form content.

Which product should lead the launch?

Most brands should not push the entire catalog first. We will identify the SKU or bundle most likely to create early signal.

Can the numbers support creator growth?

Samples, commissions, discounts, shipping, and ad spend all affect profitability. We will pressure-test the economics before talking about scale.

Why would this work if TikTok ads to Shopify did not?

TikTok Shop keeps discovery, social proof, offer, and checkout inside the same platform. That reduces friction, but the listing and creator system still have to be right.

What does Avanta actually handle?

Creator recruitment, affiliate workflow, listing optimization, content direction, Spark Ads, reporting, and the weekly operating decisions that keep the channel moving.
