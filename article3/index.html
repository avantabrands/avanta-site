Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1 to 30)

Phase 1 · Days 1 to 30

Shop setup, creator outreach, first videos live

Weeks 1 to 4

Week one is operational. We connect your product catalog to TikTok Shop, configure the affiliate program, set commission rates, and build the creator brief from your brand assets and product details.

From week two, outreach runs daily. We target creators in your category with a history of posting consistently and generating actual sales, not just impressions. Product ships to accepted creators alongside a brief covering hooks, what to emphasize, and what to avoid.

  • TikTok Shop account connected and configured
  • Product listings optimized for the Shop tab
  • Affiliate commission structure live
  • Creator brief built and reviewed by you
  • 50 to 100 creators in the outreach pipeline
  • First creator content live by days 14 to 21

Phase 2: Pattern Hunt (Days 31 to 60)

Phase 2 · Days 31 to 60

Finding what converts and building the playbook

Weeks 5 to 8

By this point there are 30 to 60 videos live across different creators. Most generate modest results. A smaller group converts significantly better. The job in phase two is understanding exactly why.

We analyze every performing video: the hook format, the language used, the product angle, the creator profile. These findings become your Content Playbook. Every new creator from this point gets briefed from the playbook, not from scratch. Results compound because we're repeating what works.

  • Creator roster expanding, 100 or more total in outreach
  • Top 10 to 15 performing creators identified
  • Content Playbook built from real performance data
  • New creator briefs updated with what's working
  • First Spark Ad candidates identified from organic winners
  • Weekly performance reports every Saturday

Phase 3 · Days 61 to 90

New creators continue to enter the roster, all briefed from the playbook. The system gets more efficient every week because each iteration builds on what the last one proved.

What your involvement looks like week to week

This is a full-service operation. You are not project managing creators, chasing shipments, or writing briefs. Here is your actual time commitment.

Your time commitment, honestly

Weekly call (30 minutes)

Review the performance report. Approve or flag any strategy changes. That is the full commitment on a normal week.

Product shipment approvals

We confirm sample batches before they go out. You approve the list. We handle fulfillment, tracking, and follow-up.

Brief sign-off at onboarding

You review the creator brief once at the start. From there it updates automatically based on what's working.

Slack for anything urgent

A dedicated channel for performance updates and fast decisions. Most weeks you won't need it. It's there when you do.
