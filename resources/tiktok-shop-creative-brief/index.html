TikTok Shop creative brief template for UGC and affiliate content.

A working brief for creators, operators, and internal teams who need aligned content without flattening the creator's voice.

Brief fields to include

  • Product name and one-sentence positioning
  • Target buyer and purchase trigger
  • Three opening hooks
  • Demonstration sequence or proof points
  • Offer details and CTA
  • Compliance notes
  • Examples of what good looks like
What a good brief does A clear brief reduces confusion, speeds up production, and gives creators enough direction to deliver content that still feels natural on-platform.
