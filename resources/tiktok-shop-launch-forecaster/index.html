How to use the TikTok Shop launch forecaster

Start with your real AOV, product cost, commission, and fulfillment cost. Then pressure-test creator sample volume and ad spend. If net profit gets worse as GMV rises, the issue is usually contribution margin, sample efficiency, or paid ROAS.

What the output means

GMV shows launch scale. Profit shows whether the operating model can support that scale. Breakeven GMV shows how much monthly TikTok Shop volume is needed before fixed launch costs stop dragging the model down.

Best next step

Use this forecast as a first pass, then compare it against inventory, fulfillment capacity, creator pipeline quality, listing conversion, and cash timing before making a final launch budget decision.
