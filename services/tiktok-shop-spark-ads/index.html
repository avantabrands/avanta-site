Where Spark Ads fit in the TikTok Shop system

Paid media works best once the creator pipeline is already producing native-feeling assets. The goal is not to replace affiliate growth. It is to identify content with strong watch-through, click intent, and conversion behavior, then use spend to accelerate distribution around those proven signals.

For most brands, that means three steps. First, build enough organic and affiliate content volume to spot patterns. Second, isolate creators and hooks with repeatable demand. Third, deploy Spark Ads on the assets that have already shown they can move product.
