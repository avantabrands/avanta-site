Where new brands usually go wrong

The early mistakes on TikTok Shop are rarely about the product. They are about entering without infrastructure: no creator pipeline, no Spark Ads strategy, listings that do not convert, and no clear read on what is actually working. Brands that rush the setup spend months correcting the foundation instead of scaling on top of it.

Setting up before building

A TikTok Shop account is not a channel strategy. Brands that treat the setup as the launch skip the work that makes the channel produce: creator relationships, proven hooks, and a content engine that compounds over time.

Choosing reach over fit

Chasing high-follower creators early is one of the fastest ways to burn budget without results. The brands that win in the first 90 days build with micro and mid-tier creators who have real audience trust, not just reach.
