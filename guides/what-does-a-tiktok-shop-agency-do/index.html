What does a TikTok Shop agency actually do?

A real TikTok Shop agency should not just run ads or send samples. It should help a brand build a complete growth system across creators, content, listings, paid amplification, and performance feedback loops.

The short answer

A strong agency helps with launch planning, creator outreach, affiliate activation, content briefing, listing optimization, paid amplification, reporting, and ongoing iteration. Some agencies only cover one or two of those layers. The stronger ones understand how they connect.

What good looks like

Clear service definitions, proof pages that show outcomes, and enough operational depth to explain what happens after the kickoff call.

How Avanta breaks it down

Separate pages for management, creator growth, and Spark Ads because each layer matters.
