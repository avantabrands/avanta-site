The three pricing models explained

1. Monthly Retainer

You pay a fixed fee every month regardless of results. This is the most common model. It works well when the agency delivers consistent value, but creates misalignment — the agency gets paid even if your shop stalls.

2. Commission on GMV

The agency earns a percentage of sales they generate (typically 5-15%). This aligns incentives better, but can encourage short-term volume over sustainable growth. It also requires clean attribution tracking.

3. Performance-Based

The agency earns based on hitting specific revenue targets. This is the most aligned model but also the riskiest for the agency — which is why only confident operators offer it. Avanta Brands uses a performance-aligned model.

4. Hybrid (Retainer + Commission)

A base fee covers fixed costs (team time, tools) plus a performance bonus tied to GMV milestones. This balances risk for both sides.

Hidden costs most brands miss

  • Creator samples — Sending free product to 50-100 creators costs money. Budget $500-2,000/month depending on product cost.
  • Ad spend — Spark Ads budget is almost always separate from agency fees. Plan $2,000-10,000/month in ad spend.
  • Content production — Some agencies charge extra for video editing or UGC coordination.
  • Shop setup — If your shop is not live yet, some agencies charge a one-time setup fee.
  • Additional SKUs — Managing 5 products is not 5x the work of 1 product, but it is more work. Some agencies charge per SKU after a threshold.

When is an agency worth the investment?

An agency is worth it when the cost of learning yourself exceeds the agency fee. Most brands waste 3-6 months and $10K-30K in trial-and-error before finding a working system. A good agency brings a proven system on day one.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a TikTok Shop agency cost per month?+
Most TikTok Shop agencies charge between $2,500 and $15,000 per month. Entry-level services start around $2,500-$5,000. Full-service management typically runs $5,000-$10,000. Premium agencies with performance guarantees can charge $10,000-$15,000+.
What pricing models do TikTok Shop agencies use?+
The three most common models are: 1) Monthly retainer — flat fee for services; 2) Commission — percentage of GMV generated; 3) Performance-based — fee tied to specific revenue milestones. Some agencies use a hybrid of retainer + commission.
Is a performance-based TikTok Shop agency better?+
Performance-based agencies align incentives with your growth. If the agency only earns when you earn, they are motivated to drive real GMV, not just activity. Avanta Brands offers a performance-aligned model with a guarantee: scale to $100K in 90 days or you do not pay.
What affects TikTok Shop agency pricing?+
Pricing depends on: number of SKUs managed, creator volume needed, ad spend managed, content production requirements, shop setup complexity, and whether the agency specializes in your vertical.
Are there hidden costs when working with a TikTok Shop agency?+
Watch for: separate ad management fees, creator sample budgets not included in the retainer, setup fees for shop creation, and charges for additional SKUs beyond the agreed scope. Always ask what is included and what costs extra before signing.
