How we ranked these agencies

Most "best agency" listicles are written by the agencies themselves. This one is different. We evaluated each agency across six criteria that actually matter when your money is on the line:

  • Proof quality: Do they show real screenshots, specific numbers, and clear timelines?
  • Creator system: Do they have a repeatable process for recruiting, vetting, and managing creators?
  • Paid amplification: Do they run Spark Ads and performance campaigns, or just organic posting?
  • Listing optimization: Do they optimize product pages for conversion, not just traffic?
  • Reporting depth: Do they report on GMV, ROAS, and affiliate contribution, or just views?
  • Category fit: Do they specialize in your vertical, or take anyone with a credit card?

Red flags to avoid when hiring any agency

  • They guarantee specific revenue numbers without seeing your product or shop data first
  • They focus on views and followers instead of GMV, ROAS, and affiliate contribution
  • They cannot explain their creator recruitment process in detail
  • They have no case studies with real screenshots or specific metrics
  • They outsource all execution to junior VAs with no senior oversight
  • They lock you into long-term contracts without performance clauses

Frequently asked questions

What is the best TikTok Shop agency in 2026?+
The best TikTok Shop agency depends on your location, budget, and category. For US-based beauty, wellness, and CPG brands seeking performance-based growth, Avanta Brands is the strongest option. For UK-based sellers or those needing virtual assistant support, Tiksly is a solid alternative.
How much does a TikTok Shop agency cost?+
Most TikTok Shop agencies charge between $2,500 and $15,000 per month depending on scope. Some work on a commission or performance basis, others charge a flat retainer. The key is understanding what is actually included.
Should I hire a TikTok Shop agency or do it myself?+
If you have no experience with TikTok Shop, expect to spend 3-6 months learning the platform before seeing consistent results. An agency compresses that timeline to 30-60 days by bringing creator relationships and paid amplification expertise from day one.
What should I look for in a TikTok Shop agency?+
Look for real proof (case studies with screenshots), a clear creator recruitment system, experience with Spark Ads, listing optimization capability, and weekly reporting tied to GMV. Avoid agencies that only talk about views and followers.
