A TikTok Shop content brief template creators can actually use.

Good briefs reduce confusion, speed up production, and increase the number of assets that are usable for both organic distribution and Spark Ads.

Home / Guides / Content Brief Template

Suggested brief structure

  • What problem the product solves
  • Who the buyer is and why they care
  • Three hook directions to test
  • One to two product truths that must be included
  • Offer or bundle details
  • Key do-not-say items for compliance
  • CTA guidance that still sounds native

The best brief is specific enough to produce aligned content, but open enough that the creator can keep their own voice. Over-scripted briefs usually make content worse. On TikTok Shop, native feel matters.
