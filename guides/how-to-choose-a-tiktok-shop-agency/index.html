How to choose a TikTok Shop agency without wasting months.

The best evaluation framework is simple: does the agency show clear proof, category understanding, a creator system, a paid strategy, and a believable process for turning early chaos into stable learning?

Home / Guides / How To Choose a TikTok Shop Agency

Ask for proof

Look for screenshots, case studies, metrics, and concrete descriptions of what was actually done.

Ask what layers they own

If they only do paid or only do creators, ask who handles the gaps that usually block scale.

Ask about category fit

Beauty, supplements, Amazon-native brands, and Shopify brands have different operating realities.

Ask about reporting

The right agency should explain how it knows what to scale, what to cut, and what to test next.
